Contributor Guide¶

This project welcomes contributions and suggestions. Check the sections below to find out how to connect to our community of users and developers. Also make sure to read and respect our community’s Code of conduct. The contribution guide explains how to structure your contributions. Please reach out if you encouter any issues.

Real-time communication¶

For real time communication please join us on Gitter. All are welcome!

To report bugs, request features, or ask usage questions please use the appropriate channels as outlined on GitHub.

Community calls¶

We have a monthly contributor meeting every second Thursday of the month at 11am New York City time. Check what this means in your time zone with Time.is. All are welcome! Please reach out via Gitter and we will forward the invitation to you. We keep notes from each meeting in our HackMD.
