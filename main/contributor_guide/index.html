Real-time communication#

For real time communication please join us on Discord. All are welcome!

To report bugs, request features, or ask usage questions please use the appropriate channels as outlined on GitHub.

Community calls#

We have a weekly community call every Thursday at 11am US Eastern time (ET). Check what this means in your time zone with Time.is. All are welcome! Please reach out via Discord and we will forward the invitation to you. We keep notes from each meeting in our HackMD.
